Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to PDF Tools. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services.

Information We Process

Our PDF tools process your files directly in your browser. We do not store or transmit your files to any server. All processing is done locally on your device.

  • We don't collect personal information
  • Files are processed entirely in your browser
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • No cookies are used for tracking

How We Use Your Information

Since all processing is done locally:

  • Your files remain private and secure
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • We have no access to your documents
  • Files are automatically cleared when you close the page

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All processing is done client-side
  • No server storage or transmission
  • Uses secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
